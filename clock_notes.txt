MSP430X5XXXX
	5 clock sources
		1) XT1CLK: low or high frequency oscillator for external source
		2) VLOCLK: internal very low power oscillator (~10kHz)
		3) REFCLOCK: internal trimmed, 32768 typical frequency
		4) DCOCLK: internal digitally controlled oscillator
		5) XT2CLK: optional high frequerncy oscillator for external source

	3 signals available:
		1) ACLK
		2) MCLK
		3) SMCLK
MSP430X2XXXX
	2/3/4 clock sources:
		1) LFXT1CLK: low frequency oscillator for external source
		2) XT2CLK: optional high frequency oscillator for external source
		3) DCLOCK: internal digitally controlled oscillator
		4) VLOCLK: internal low power, low frequency oscillator (~12kHz)
	
	3 signals available: 
		1) ACLK
		2) MCLK
		3) SMCLK
